Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook diced pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Reduce heat to low. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to combine with the pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti while tossing continuously to prevent the eggs from scrambling. Add reserved pasta water gradually to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired. Serve immediately.

Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
